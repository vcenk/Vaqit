## Messaging

**Hero:** "Prayer alerts you can verify." (Alt: "Know your athan is ready.")

Three proof pillars — each demonstrable in-product, none dependent on competitors staying broken:
1. **Verified readiness** — home-screen "Alerts ready — next: Fajr 4:12 AM"; Health Check; delivery ledger; shareable diagnostics. *We publish our measured reliability stats instead of claiming perfection.*
2. **Explained times** — tap any time → method, angles, source, adjustments; "why is my app 10 minutes different from my mosque?" answered in-product with the mosque comparison view.
3. **Honest privacy** — minimal permissions, pre-prompt explanations, manual-city path, and a public "every network request" page. Precise promise: your location, prayer history, and settings stay on-device; optional online features disclose what they send.

**Never say:** "never fails," "guaranteed delivery," "100% accurate," "nothing ever leaves your phone," or name-and-shame competitors in store assets. The landing page proves what the app does; content marketing may discuss category problems (data scandals, ads in worship) without brand attacks.

## Channels (ranked)

### 1. ASO
- EN keywords: prayer times, athan, adhan, qibla, salah, namaz, ramadan 2027, iqamah; TR (v1.1): namaz vakitleri, ezan, kıble, sahur, iftar
- Screenshots: assurance status → tap-a-time source card → mosque comparison → Health Check (show the product's proof, not adjective claims)
- Ratings prompt only after a positive moment (successful Health Check, week of logged prayers); respond to every review (n8n pipeline); the diagnostic report turns 1-star "athan didn't fire" reviews into solvable support cases — publicly visible competence
- **Android emphasis**: the underserved flank (competitor Android ratings lag iOS; battery-saver pain is Android-native and our diagnostics answer it)

### 2. Reddit + communities
- r/islam, r/MuslimLounge, r/converts, city subs; honest builder narrative: "I got tired of guessing whether my Fajr alarm would fire, so I built verification into a prayer app." Transparency posts with real reliability data. Never astroturf.
- Beta recruitment via mosque WhatsApp groups (Vancouver/Coquitlam network), Turkish community contacts — skew Samsung users deliberately

### 3. Mosque partnerships (moat, redefined vs MAWAQIT)
- MAWAQIT already owns "mosque-managed times" at global scale (130+ countries, nonprofit, free). Do NOT pitch mosques "switch platforms."
- Our pitch: **provenance layer** — verified profile with source, last-verified date, effective range, version history, and the calculation-diff explanation their congregants keep asking about. Works alongside whatever the mosque already uses; timetable import is fine with permission.
- Target: 5–10 verified Lower Mainland mosques at launch, 10+ by Ramadan, 50 by end-2027; QR flyers at Jumu'ah/iftar

### 4. Short-form content (n8n-automated, batch Sundays)
- "Why your Fajr alarm didn't go off on Samsung — and how to prove it's fixed"
- "Why your app and your mosque disagree by 10 minutes" (method/angle explainers — P10 content is infinite and searchable)
- High-latitude explainers (Vancouver/London summer Fajr) — zero competition, high intent
- Turkish versions of top performers (v1.1)

### 5. Micro-influencers
- Muslim productivity/lifestyle creators 10–100K; gift Supporter + sponsored-pool codes; Turkish diaspora creators at TR launch

### 6. PR (opportunistic)
- Angle: "A Vancouver engineer built a prayer app that shows its work — every time explained, every alert verifiable." Pitch local (CBC/Global BC), Muslim media (Amaliah, MuslimMatters), tech (BetaKit). Privacy + Ramadan seasonal hooks for App Store featuring pitch (submit early Jan).
