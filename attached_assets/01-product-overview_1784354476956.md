# 01 — Product Overview

**Project codename:** Vakit (working name — TM/App Store check required; alternatives: Sabit, Miqat, Salli, NoorTime)
**Owner:** Cenk
**Status:** Pre-MVP planning
**Last updated:** 2026-07-17

## One-liner

The prayer app that never fails you: bulletproof athan notifications, accurate times at any latitude, zero ads in worship, and your data never leaves your phone.

## Vision

Become the default prayer companion for Muslims in Western countries by winning on **reliability and trust** — the two things the market leaders have proven they cannot deliver.

## Why now

- Muslim Pro (~180M downloads) has permanent trust damage (2020 data-selling scandal) and monetizes with intrusive ads inside worship features — including, documented in reviews, ads for other religions.
- Pillars (the "privacy-first" challenger) validated the positioning but is plagued by notification bugs on Android, stuck-location bugs, wrong calculation angles, and a truncated athan on iOS.
- Faith apps are proven revenue: Hallow did ~$40M net revenue in 2025 in the Christian segment. No Muslim app has executed the Hallow-quality product + habit playbook.
- Ramadan 2027 (~Feb 2027) is a predictable, massive organic download spike — a free acquisition window if we launch 6–8 weeks before.

## Positioning statement

For practicing Muslims in Canada, the US, and the UK who have been burned by ad-stuffed or unreliable prayer apps, **Vakit** is a prayer companion that treats notifications as a mission-critical system, prayer-time accuracy as a religious obligation, and user data as untouchable — unlike Muslim Pro (ads + trust scandal) and Pillars (buggy execution).

## Core principles (non-negotiable)

1. **Worship is free forever.** Athan, prayer times, qibla, Quran text — never paywalled, never interrupted by ads.
2. **No ads. Ever.** Not "no ads in premium." No ads.
3. **Data stays on device.** No location upload, no analytics SDKs that track, no third-party data sharing. Provable and loudly stated.
4. **Notifications are the product.** If the athan doesn't fire, nothing else matters. Engineering priority #1.
5. **We answer users.** Public roadmap, in-app feature voting, replies to reviews. The incumbents' silence is our marketing.

## Target market

**Primary:** Practicing Muslim millennials/Gen Z (18–40) in Western countries — Canada, US, UK first.
- ~4M Muslims in North America; ~6M English-speaking in UK/EU.
- Pay-for-apps culture; privacy-aware; burned by Muslim Pro.
- High-latitude locations make them the worst-served on accuracy (Fajr/Isha angle problems) — and our best-served.

**Secondary (v1.1 via Turkish localization):** Turkish diaspora (Germany ~3M, plus NL/FR/CA/UK) and Turkey. Diyanet calculation method support day one. Founder's bilingual advantage.

**Persona:** 28-year-old professional in Vancouver/Toronto/London. Prays 5x daily or rebuilding consistency. Deleted Muslim Pro after the scandal. Currently: basic athan app + paper qaza notes + YouTube tafsir. Wants one clean app that just works.
