# 07 — Launch & Marketing Plan

## Strategic timing

Ramadan 2027 begins ~Feb 17, 2027. Faith-app downloads spike massively around it (Hallow's Lent analog: 1.2M downloads in 22 days). Everything works backward from that date:

- **Dec 2026:** v1.0 public launch (free-only) → accumulate ratings/reviews
- **Mid-Jan 2027:** v1.1 + Ramadan Mode + Plus tier live
- **Feb 2027:** Ramadan surge capture (ASO peak season for "prayer times", "ramadan", "iftar")

## Positioning & messaging

**Hero message:** "The prayer app that never fails you."

Three proof pillars (each maps to a documented incumbent failure):
1. *"Your athan will fire. We obsess over it."* → Notification Health Check demo
2. *"Accurate where you live — even in Canada in June."* → high-latitude + mosque sync story
3. *"No ads. No tracking. Ever."* → 'every network request' transparency page

Anti-positioning (never name competitors in-store; do it in content): "built after watching prayer apps sell user data and show ads inside the Quran."

## Channels (ranked by expected ROI)

### 1. ASO (highest leverage, founder's proven skill)
- Keywords: prayer times, athan/adhan, qibla, muslim, salah tracker, ramadan 2027; TR: namaz vakitleri, ezan, kıble
- Screenshots lead with reliability + privacy claims, not feature grids
- Ratings strategy: in-app prompt only after a streak milestone (happy moment); respond to every review (n8n pipeline)

### 2. Reddit + communities (trust-building, zero cost)
- r/islam, r/MuslimLounge, r/converts, city subs (r/vancouver Muslims etc.)
- Play: honest builder posts ("I got burned by prayer app data scandals, so I built…"), beta invites, transparency updates. Never astroturf.
- Discord/WhatsApp mosque groups for beta cohort (Vancouver/Coquitlam network incl. existing community contacts)

### 3. Short-form content (n8n-automated)
- TikTok/IG Reels/Shorts: "why your athan doesn't go off on Samsung (and how we fixed it)", high-latitude Fajr explainers, Ramadan prep tips
- Reuse existing FAL/OpenAI/n8n content pipelines; batch Sundays
- Turkish-language versions of top performers → underserved TR diaspora content niche

### 4. Mosque partnerships (moat-building)
- Offer free timetable hosting to 5–10 Vancouver/Lower Mainland mosques pre-Ramadan; flyer/QR at iftar events
- Each pinned mosque = organic local distribution + retention lock-in

### 5. Micro-influencers
- Muslim lifestyle/productivity creators (10–100K followers); gift Plus + sponsored pool codes rather than cash initially
- Turkish diaspora creators for the TR push

### 6. PR angle (opportunistic)
- Story: "After the Muslim Pro data scandal, a Vancouver engineer built a prayer app that can't spy on you." Pitch: local CBC/Global BC, Muslim media (Amaliah, MuslimMatters), tech (BetaKit)
