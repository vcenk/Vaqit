# 05 — Monetization

## Rule zero

**Worship is never monetized.** Athan, prayer times, qibla, tracker, Quran text, Ramadan basics: free forever, no ads, no limits. This rule is the brand. Violating it recreates the exact resentment (P3/P7) we exist to fix.

## Model: Freemium subscription (+ optional supporter tier)

### Free (forever)

Everything in MVP: F1–F6, F8, Ramadan mode core, Quran reader (v1.1).

### Premium — "Vakit Plus" @ $3.99/mo or $29.99/yr (intro: $24.99/yr)

Deliberately undercuts Muslim Pro Premium ($12.99/mo!) and Hallow ($9.99/mo). Price signals fairness; volume + goodwill > ARPU at this stage.

Included (all genuinely *additive*, not core worship):
1. **Qaza Tracker & Planner (F9)** — calculate missed-prayer debt, structured make-up plan, progress. High emotional value; nobody does it well.
2. **AI Companion (F10)** — tafsir Q&A grounded in vetted sources (with scholar-review disclaimer), situational dua finder, "explain this ayah" in EN/TR.
3. **Memorization Coach (F11)** — recite, Deepgram speech recognition scores accuracy, spaced-repetition review queue.
4. **Advanced tracker analytics** — long-term trends, exportable journal.
5. **Full audio library offline** — multiple reciters, downloads.
6. **Premium widgets/themes** — cosmetic only; never gate the functional widget.

### Family Plan @ $59.99/yr (up to 6) — v1.3

Religious households buy together; parents gift to kids/parents. Higher LTV, lower churn.

### Sadaqah / Supporter mechanics

- **Gift a subscription**: buy Plus for someone else (or "sponsor a student").
- **Sponsored pool**: users can donate subs to a pool; anyone can request a free year, no questions asked. Cost is near-zero, PR and barakah framing is priceless, and it inoculates against "paywalling deen" criticism.
- Optional one-time "Support the app" tip jar (Hallow-adjacent goodwill; YouVersion-style non-pushy).

## What we will NEVER do

- Ads of any kind (P3).
- Sell/share any user data (P4).
- Gate athan sounds, calculation methods, widgets' core function, or notification features.
- Dark-pattern trials or hard-to-cancel subs (Muzz/Muslima reviews show the rage this creates; RevenueCat + honest cancel flow).

## Future B2B option (v2+, do not build now)

Mosque timetable portal: mosques manage published timetables + announcements pushed to congregants who pinned them. Free for mosques initially → later paid tier (announcements, events, donation links). Creates a distribution + retention moat that consumer competitors can't easily copy.
